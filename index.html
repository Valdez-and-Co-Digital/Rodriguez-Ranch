The Rodriguez Legacy

Over 170 Years of Family Farming

Established in 1856, Rodriguez Ranch has stood as a cornerstone of local Texan agriculture. What began as a traditional homestead has evolved over generations, remaining true to our values of hard work, animal welfare, and pure, unprocessed country goods.

Today, the fifth generation of the Rodriguez family manages our acreage. We keep our flock sizes manageable to ensure every laying hen enjoys open pastures, fresh air, and personal care. This focus on standard-setting animal care reflects in the unparalleled freshness of our eggs and the playful, healthy nature of the kittens born on our land.
